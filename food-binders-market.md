<p><p>Food binders are essential ingredients used in the food industry to improve texture, consistency, and stability of food products. They can be categorized into four main types: sugar binders, starch binders, protein binders, and gel binders. Sugar binders help in moisture retention and can be commonly found in cookies and cakes. Starch binders are used to thicken and stabilize sauces and soups. Protein binders aid in binding meat products together. Gel binders create a smooth and creamy texture in dairy products and desserts.</p></p>
