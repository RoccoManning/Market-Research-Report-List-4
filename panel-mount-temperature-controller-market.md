<p><p>Panel mount temperature controllers are used to regulate and control temperature in various industrial applications. Switch controllers are basic on/off controllers that turn heating or cooling systems on or off based on preset temperature limits. Proportional controllers modulate the output in proportion to the deviation from the setpoint, allowing for more precise temperature control. PID controllers use proportional, integral, and derivative control actions to maintain temperature with minimal error. Each type of controller offers different levels of precision and control in managing temperature in different environments.</p></p>
