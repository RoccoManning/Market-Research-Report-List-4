<p><strong>The Dispersive Mirror Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gires-Tournois Interferometer (GTI) Mirror</li><li>Chirped Mirror</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dispersive mirrors come in various types including Gires-Tournois Interferometer (GTI) mirrors and Chirped mirrors. GTI mirrors consist of multiple layers of dielectric materials which are designed to enhance the dispersion properties of the mirror. Chirped mirrors are designed with a varying thickness across the mirror's surface, providing controlled dispersion of light over a wide range of wavelengths. Both types of mirrors are crucial in applications requiring precise control over the dispersion of light, such as in ultrafast lasers and optical communication systems.</p></p>
