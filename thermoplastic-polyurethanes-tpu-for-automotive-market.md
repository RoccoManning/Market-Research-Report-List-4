<p><p>Thermoplastic Polyurethanes (TPU) are widely used in the automotive industry due to their excellent properties such as high flexibility, durability, and chemical resistance. There are three main types of TPU used in the automotive market: Polyester-based TPU, Polyether-based TPU, and Polycaprolactone-based TPU. Each type offers unique characteristics, with Polyester-based TPU providing good mechanical properties, Polyether-based TPU offering superior resistance to hydrolysis, and Polycaprolactone-based TPU being known for its biocompatibility and low-temperature flexibility. These TPUs cater to different automotive applications based on their specific properties.</p></p>
