<p><p>Metal deburring tools come in two main types: hand deburring tools and automatic deburring tools. Hand deburring tools are used for manual removal of burrs and sharp edges on metal surfaces, providing more precision and control over the process. Automatic deburring tools, on the other hand, are designed for mass production and consist of machinery that automatically removes burrs from metal parts. Both types of tools are essential in the metal fabrication industry to ensure smooth and safe metal surfaces.</p></p>
