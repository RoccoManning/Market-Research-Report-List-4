<p><p>Welded Steel Pipe: Welded steel pipes are made by welding steel plates together to form a cylindrical shape. They are commonly used in construction, oil and gas transportation, and structural applications. Welded steel pipes are cost-effective and versatile, able to withstand high pressure and temperature.</p><p>Seamless Steel Pipe: Seamless steel pipes are made by piercing a solid steel billet to create a hollow tube without any seams. They are known for their uniform structure, high strength, and smooth surface finish. Seamless steel pipes are commonly used in industries requiring high precision and strength, such as petrochemicals, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
