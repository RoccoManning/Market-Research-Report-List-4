<p><p>Voltage clamp modules are used in electrophysiology experiments to control the membrane potential of a cell. Single electrode voltage clamp modules use one electrode to control the membrane potential, while two electrode voltage clamp modules use two electrodes with one controlling the membrane potential and the other measuring the current. Single electrode voltage clamp is simpler and easier to set up, while two electrode voltage clamp offers more precise control over the membrane potential. Both types are essential tools for studying ion channels and membrane physiology.</p></p>
