<p><p>Amphibious dredgers are versatile machines used for dredging operations in both shallow waters and on land. The three main types of amphibious dredgers are Cutter-suction Dredgers, Trailing Suction Hopper Dredgers, and Others. Cutter-suction dredgers use a rotating cutter head to loosen and remove material, while Trailing Suction Hopper Dredgers pump sediment into onboard hoppers for transportation and disposal. Other types of amphibious dredgers include Bucket Wheel Dredgers and Water Injection Dredgers, each with unique capabilities for various dredging tasks.</p></p>
