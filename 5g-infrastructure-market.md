<p><p>5G infrastructure market includes different types of cell sizes catering to varying coverage areas. Femtocells are small, low-power base stations for indoor use in residential or small business settings. Pico cells cover larger indoor areas like shopping malls or airports. Micro cells provide coverage for urban areas and high-traffic public spaces. Macro cells are the largest cells, covering wide outdoor areas like cities and rural areas. Each type of cell plays a crucial role in providing seamless and efficient 5G connectivity.</p></p>
