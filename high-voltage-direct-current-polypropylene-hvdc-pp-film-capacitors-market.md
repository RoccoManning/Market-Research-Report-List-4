<p><p>High Voltage Direct Current Polypropylene (HVDC PP) Film Capacitors are used in two main types of HVDC systems: Voltage Source Converters (VSC) and Line Commutated Converters (LCC). </p><p>VSC systems utilize capacitors to smooth out the voltage and provide a stable output, making them suitable for renewable energy integration and asynchronous connections. </p><p>On the other hand, LCC systems require capacitors with higher energy storage capabilities to support the commutation process and maintain system stability during faults, making them ideal for long-distance transmission lines and interconnections.</p></p>
