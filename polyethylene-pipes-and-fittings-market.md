<p><p>Polyethylene pipes and fittings market types include Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE), which is flexible and commonly used for irrigation and potable water systems. Linear Low Density Polyethylene (LLDPE) is known for its toughness and is often used in gas and water distribution. High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) is strong and resistant to chemicals, making it ideal for sewer and drainage systems. Other types of polyethylene pipes and fittings may include Medium Density Polyethylene (MDPE) and Cross-linked Polyethylene (PEX), each offering different strengths and properties for various applications.</p></p>
