<p><p>Straight infusion connectors are medical devices used to connect infusion sets to IV catheters. They come in various types, including those with hemostasis valves to prevent blood loss, those with stopcocks to control flow, and other variations. Connectors with hemostasis valves are designed to maintain a closed system and reduce the risk of contamination. Connectors with stopcocks allow for easy control of fluid flow. Other types of connectors may offer additional features or capabilities for specific medical applications.</p></p>
