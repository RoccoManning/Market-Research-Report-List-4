<p><p>Commercial Acousto-Optic Tunable Filters (AOTFs) are advanced optical devices used for spectral filtering in various applications. There are two main types of AOTFs in the market - Collinear AOTFs and Noncollinear AOTFs. Collinear AOTFs are designed with the acoustic and optical beams traveling in the same direction, allowing for high efficiency and narrow bandwidth filtering. Noncollinear AOTFs, on the other hand, have the acoustic and optical beams traveling at different angles, offering wider bandwidth and tunability options for different filtering needs.</p></p>
