<p><p>Gravure ink market types include water-based, alcoholic-based, benzene-based, and petrol-based options. Water-based gravure ink is eco-friendly and easy to clean, while alcoholic-based ink offers fast drying times. Benzene-based ink provides excellent print quality but raises environmental concerns. Petrol-based ink is known for its high performance, but it may be harmful to the environment. Each type of gravure ink serves different purposes and caters to specific needs in the printing industry, offering a range of options for manufacturers to choose from.</p></p>
