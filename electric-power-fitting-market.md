<p><p>The Electric Power Fitting Market includes various types such as Suspension Clamp, Strain Clamp, Link fitting, Protective fitting, and Others. Suspension clamps are used to hang conductor or ground wire on the tower. Strain clamps are used to secure the conductor in tension. Link fittings connect two conductors or earth wires. Protective fittings provide insulation and protection for power lines. Other fittings include various components like spacers, shackles, and spindles that are crucial for the efficient functioning of electric power systems.</p></p>
