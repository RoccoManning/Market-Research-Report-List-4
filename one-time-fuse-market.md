<p><p>One-Time Fuse Market Types include Quick Disconnect Fuses and Slow-Breaking Fuses. Quick Disconnect Fuses are designed to disconnect quickly when the current exceeds a certain level, providing fast protection for equipment. Slow-Breaking Fuses, on the other hand, have a delayed response to overcurrent situations, allowing for temporary power surges while still offering protection against sustained high currents. Both types of fuses cater to different needs in terms of speed and level of protection required in various electrical systems.</p></p>
