<p><strong>The Titanium Dioxide Pigment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sulfate Process</li><li>Chloride Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Titanium dioxide pigment market is divided into two main types: Sulfate Process and Chloride Process. The Sulfate Process involves treating titanium ore with sulfuric acid to produce titanium dioxide, while the Chloride Process involves using chlorine gas to transform titanium tetrachloride into titanium dioxide. The Sulfate Process is more commonly used due to its lower cost and simpler production process, while the Chloride Process is known for producing higher quality pigment with better dispersion properties.</p></p>
