<p><p>Contactless IC card chips can be divided into two main types: Single Chip and Dual Chip markets. In the Single Chip market, the chip integrates all necessary functions in one chip, reducing the overall size and cost of the card. On the other hand, the Dual Chip market involves two separate chips: one for processing and another for security, providing enhanced security features but also increasing the complexity and cost of production. Both types cater to different requirements and preferences in the contactless IC card industry.</p></p>
