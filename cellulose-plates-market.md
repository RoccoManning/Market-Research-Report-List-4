TLC involves the separation of compounds on a thin layer of cellulose, while HPTLC offers enhanced performance with improved resolution and sensitivity. Both techniques are cost-effective, simple to use, and offer quick results, making them popular choices in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, food analysis, and environmental testing.
