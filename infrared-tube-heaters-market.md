<p><p>Infrared tube heaters are commonly found in two types in the market - Two-Stage Infrared Tube Heaters and Single-Stage Infrared Tube Heaters. On the other hand, Single-Stage Infrared Tube Heaters offer a single level of heat output, making them suitable for maintaining consistent temperatures in smaller spaces or environments where a fixed heat level is sufficient.</p></p>
