<p><p>AISG connectors are categorized into two main types: Plug AISG connectors and Socket AISG connectors. Plug AISG connectors are male connectors that have pins which are inserted into the female socket AISG connectors for a secure connection. Socket AISG connectors, on the other hand, are female connectors that have receptacles to receive the pins from the plug connectors. Both types of connectors are essential components in AISG systems for the transmission of data and power.</p></p>
