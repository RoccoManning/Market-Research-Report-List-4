<p><strong>The Hydrogen Generation by Water Electrolysis Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Alkaline Water Electrolysis</li><li>Proton Exchange membrane (PEM) Electrolysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydrogen generation by water electrolysis market is primarily divided into two types: Alkaline Water Electrolysis and Proton Exchange membrane (PEM) Electrolysis. </p><p>Alkaline Water Electrolysis involves the use of alkaline electrolyte solution to produce hydrogen gas. This method is cost-effective and has been widely used for many years.</p><p>On the other hand, Proton Exchange membrane (PEM) Electrolysis utilizes a solid polymer electrolyte membrane to separate the hydrogen and oxygen gases. This method is more efficient, requires lower energy input, and is suitable for on-site hydrogen generation applications.</p></p>
