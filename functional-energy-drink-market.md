<p><strong>The Functional Energy Drink Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isotonic</li><li>Hypertonic</li><li>Hypotonic</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Functional energy drinks come in different market types based on their composition and intended effects on the body. Isotonic drinks have a similar concentration of salt and sugar as the body's cells, making them easily absorbed and replenishing lost fluids during exercise. Hypertonic drinks have a higher concentration of salt and sugar, providing a quick energy boost but may be slower to hydrate. Hypotonic drinks have a lower concentration of salt and sugar, quickly replenishing fluids but may not provide as much energy. Other market types may include specialized formulas targeting specific health benefits.</p></p>
