<p><strong>The Flux Cored Welding Wire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas-shielded</li><li>Self-shielded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flux cored welding wire comes in two main types: gas-shielded and self-shielded. Gas-shielded flux-cored wire requires an external shielding gas to protect the weld pool from atmospheric contamination, while self-shielded wire generates its own protective gas when heated. Gas-shielded wire is generally preferred for welding thicker materials and outdoor applications where wind can affect shielding gas coverage. Self-shielded wire, on the other hand, is more portable and convenient as it eliminates the need for an external gas supply, making it ideal for field work and quick repairs.</p></p>
