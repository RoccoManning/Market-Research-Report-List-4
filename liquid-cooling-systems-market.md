<p><p>Liquid Cooling Systems market includes two main types: Liquid Heat Exchanger System and Compressor-based System. </p><p>Liquid Heat Exchanger System uses a liquid coolant to absorb and dissipate heat from electronic components, transferring it to a heat exchanger where it is cooled before being recirculated. </p><p>Compressor-based System, on the other hand, utilizes a compressor to cool the liquid coolant before circulating it through the system, providing more efficient cooling for high-performance applications. Both systems are commonly used in data centers, industrial machinery, and electronic devices.</p></p>
