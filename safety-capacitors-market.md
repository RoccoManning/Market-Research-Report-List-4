<p><strong>The Safety Capacitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Class-X Capacitors</li><li>Class-Y Capacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Safety capacitors are used in electrical circuits to minimize the risk of electrical shock and fire hazards. Class-X capacitors are specifically designed for electromagnetic interference suppression in AC power lines, while Class-Y capacitors are used for electromagnetic interference suppression in the line-to-ground and line-to-line applications. Class-X capacitors are further categorized into X1, X2, and X3 depending on their voltage ratings, while Class-Y capacitors are categorized into Y1, Y2, Y3, and Y4 based on their peak pulse voltage levels.</p></p>
