<p><p>Baby play mats are available in various types such as PE, PVC, EVA, and others. PE play mats are made of polyethylene and are known for their durability and eco-friendly nature. PVC play mats are made of polyvinyl chloride and are waterproof and easy to clean. EVA play mats are made of ethylene-vinyl acetate and are soft and cushioned, making them ideal for babies to play on. Other types of play mats may include foam or fabric materials. Each type offers different features and benefits for babies' safety and comfort.</p></p>
