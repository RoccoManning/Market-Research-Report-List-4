<p><p>Diaphragm filter presses come in two main types - rubber diaphragm type and polymer elastomer diaphragm type. The rubber diaphragm type is known for its durability and resistance to high temperatures and chemicals, making it ideal for industries that require heavy-duty filtration. On the other hand, polymer elastomer diaphragm types have a higher flexibility and resilience, making them suitable for applications where a more adaptable diaphragm is needed. Both types cater to different market needs and preferences within the filtration industry.</p></p>
