<p><p>Stacker cranes are commonly used in automated warehouses to transport and store goods. There are two main types of stacker cranes: Monocolumn Stacker Cranes and Two-column Stacker Cranes. Monocolumn stacker cranes have a single column supporting the crane and are suitable for smaller warehouses with limited space. Two-column stacker cranes have two columns for additional stability and are used in larger warehouses with higher storage capacity. Both types of stacker cranes offer efficient and space-saving solutions for warehouse operations.</p></p>
