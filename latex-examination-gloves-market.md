<p><p>Latex examination gloves are available in two main types in the market: powdered and powder-free. Powdered gloves have a light coating of cornstarch or other powders to make them easier to put on and take off, while powder-free gloves do not have any added powders. Both types offer protection against germs and contaminants, but some prefer powder-free gloves for avoiding potential allergic reactions or leaving a residue on surfaces. The choice between the two types depends on personal preference and specific needs.</p></p>
