<p><strong>The EVA Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normal EVA</li><li>Anti-PID EVA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Normal EVA film is a commonly used material in the photovoltaic industry for laminating solar modules due to its excellent transparency and adhesion properties. Anti-PID EVA film is specifically designed to prevent potential induced degradation in solar panels, offering better durability and performance in harsh environmental conditions. While normal EVA film meets the basic requirements for solar module encapsulation, anti-PID EVA film provides an added layer of protection against potential degradation, making it a more reliable choice for long-lasting solar panels.</p></p>
