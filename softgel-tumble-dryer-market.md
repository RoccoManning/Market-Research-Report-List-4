<p><p>Standard tumble drying in the softgel industry refers to the traditional method of drying softgel capsules in batches using a tumble dryer. This process involves loading a certain amount of softgels into the dryer, setting the temperature and time, and allowing them to tumble dry until they reach the desired moisture content.</p><p>On the other hand, continuous tumble drying is a more advanced and efficient method where softgels are dried continuously in a continuous tumble dryer. This process allows for a higher production capacity and more consistent drying results compared to standard tumble drying.</p></p>
