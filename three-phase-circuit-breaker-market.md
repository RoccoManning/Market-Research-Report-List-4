<p><p>The three-phase circuit breaker market includes two main types: manual reset and automatic reset. Manual reset circuit breakers require users to manually reset the breaker after it trips due to an overload or short circuit. On the other hand, automatic reset circuit breakers will automatically reset themselves after a trip once the fault is cleared. Both types offer protection against electrical faults, with manual reset breakers requiring human intervention while automatic reset breakers offer a more convenient solution.</p></p>
