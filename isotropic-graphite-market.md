<p><p>Isotropic Graphite Market Types - CIP Method and Vibration Molding Method are two common techniques used in the production of isotropic graphite. The CIP Method involves using a cold isostatic press to compact graphite powder into a desired shape, while the Vibration Molding Method uses vibration to achieve the same result. Both methods are used in the manufacturing of high-quality isotropic graphite products with consistent properties and performance. Each method offers its own advantages and may be preferred based on specific production requirements.</p></p>
