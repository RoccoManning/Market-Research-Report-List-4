<p><p>Waterproofing chemicals market includes various types such as waterproofing coatings, penetrating sealers, and others. Waterproofing coatings are applied on surfaces to create a protective barrier against water penetration. Penetrating sealers work by penetrating the surface and forming a protective barrier from within. Other types of waterproofing chemicals may include cementitious waterproofing, bituminous coatings, and elastomeric waterproofing. Each type of waterproofing chemical offers unique properties and is used based on the specific requirements of the surface and environment.</p></p>
