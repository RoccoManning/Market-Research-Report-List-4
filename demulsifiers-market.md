<p><strong>The Demulsifiers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil Soluble Demulsifier</li><li>Water Soluble Demulsifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Demulsifiers are mainly categorized into two types: oil soluble demulsifiers and water soluble demulsifiers. Oil soluble demulsifiers are additives that are used to separate water from oil by reducing the surface tension between the two liquids, allowing for easier and more efficient separation. On the other hand, water soluble demulsifiers are chemicals that are designed to disrupt the stability of water-in-oil emulsions by promoting coalescence and facilitating the separation process. Both types of demulsifiers play a crucial role in various industries where oil-water separation is required.</p></p>
