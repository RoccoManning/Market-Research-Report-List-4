<p><p>Glass alarm systems are available in two types: wired and wireless. Wired glass alarms are connected via physical wires to a central control panel, making installation more complex but generally more reliable. Wireless glass alarms, on the other hand, rely on radio frequency signals to communicate with the control panel, offering easier installation and flexibility in placement. Both types provide security by detecting vibrations or breaks in glass and triggering an alarm to alert homeowners or authorities of a potential intrusion.</p></p>
