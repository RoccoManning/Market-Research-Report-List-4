<p><p>The graphic processor market can be divided into two main types: dedicated graphics cards and integrated graphics processors. Dedicated graphics cards are separate components installed in a computer system solely for handling graphics processing tasks, providing high performance for demanding applications like gaming and video editing. On the other hand, integrated graphics processors are integrated into the computer's central processing unit, offering basic graphics capabilities suitable for everyday tasks like web browsing and office applications. Both types cater to different consumer needs and usage scenarios.</p></p>
