<p><p>Software Load Balancers are essential for distributing incoming network traffic across multiple servers to ensure optimal performance and availability. Installable Load Balancers are traditional software solutions installed on servers to manage traffic. On the other hand, Load Balancer as a Service (LBaaS) is a cloud-based solution where load balancer functionality is provided as a service. LBaaS offers scalability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness as users can easily adjust resources as needed without the need for dedicated hardware or software installation.</p></p>
