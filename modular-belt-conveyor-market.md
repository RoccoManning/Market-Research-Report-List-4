<p><p>Modular belt conveyor market offers two types: Open Belt Conveyors and Enclosed Belt Conveyors. Open Belt Conveyors consist of a belt stretched between two pulleys, transporting items along the conveyor. They are cost-effective and easy to maintain. Enclosed Belt Conveyors have a protective casing around the belt, preventing contamination and ensuring safe transportation of goods. They are suitable for industries requiring clean and hygienic operations. Both types offer efficiency in material handling and are widely used in various industries for their flexibility and reliability.</p></p>
