<p><p>Mobile signal boosters are devices that enhance the strength and quality of mobile signals. There are two main types of mobile signal boosters: analog signal boosters and digital signal boosters. Analog signal boosters amplify the existing analog signals, while digital signal boosters convert analog signals into digital signals for boosting. The market for analog signal boosters is more traditional and established, catering to areas with weaker analog signals. On the other hand, the digital signal booster market is growing rapidly with the shift towards digital technologies and the need for improved digital signal reception.</p></p>
