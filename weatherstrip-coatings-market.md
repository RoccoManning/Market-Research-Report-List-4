<p><strong>The Weatherstrip Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Component</li><li>Double Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Weatherstrip coatings come in two main types - Single Component and Double Component. Single Component coatings are ready to use straight from the container and do not require mixing. They are easy to apply and ideal for small-scale projects. On the other hand, Double Component coatings consist of a base component and a curing agent that need to be mixed together before application. These coatings offer higher performance and durability, making them suitable for larger and more demanding applications.</p></p>
