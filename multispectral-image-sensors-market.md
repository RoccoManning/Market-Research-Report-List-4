<p><p>Multispectral image sensors are divided into three main market types based on the type of electromagnetic spectrum they capture: visible light, near infrared (NIR), and other wavelengths beyond NIR. Visible light sensors are used for capturing images in the range of human visible spectrum, while NIR sensors are utilized for applications like agriculture, surveillance, and food inspection. Other multispectral sensors cater to specialized applications like environmental monitoring, medical imaging, and material analysis by capturing wavelengths beyond NIR range.</p></p>
