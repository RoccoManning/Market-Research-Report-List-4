<p><p>The optical engine market can be classified into various types such as DLP (Digital Light Processing), LCOS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon), LED (Light Emitting Diode), and others. DLP technology uses digital micromirror devices to produce high-quality images. LCOS technology uses liquid crystals and silicon to create sharp images with high resolution. LED technology uses light-emitting diodes for efficient and long-lasting illumination. Other technologies in the market include LBS (Laser Beam Steering) and OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode).</p></p>
