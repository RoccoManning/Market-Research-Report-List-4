<p><ul><li>Powder</li><li>Spherical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High purity activated alumina is available in two main types: powder and spherical. Powdered activated alumina is finely ground and is commonly used in applications such as gas and liquid dehydration, catalyst support, and fluoride removal. Spherical activated alumina, on the other hand, is formed into small spherical beads, providing a higher surface area for increased efficiency in applications like natural gas drying, water treatment, and air purification. Both types offer high purity and high adsorption capacity for a range of industrial processes.</p></p>
