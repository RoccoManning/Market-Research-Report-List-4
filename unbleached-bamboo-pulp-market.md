<p><p>Unbleached Bamboo Pulp Market Types refer to different methods used for processing bamboo pulp into unbleached paper products. The Sulfate Process involves treating bamboo pulp with a mixture of chemicals including sodium hydroxide and sulfide, while the Caustic Soda Process uses only caustic soda for delignification. Both processes result in unbleached bamboo pulp with different characteristics, catering to different market demands such as eco-friendly and sustainable packaging solutions. The choice of process depends on factors like cost, environmental impact, and end-use applications.</p></p>
