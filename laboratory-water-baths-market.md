<p><strong>The Laboratory Water Baths Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Circulating Water Baths</li><li>Non-Circulating Water Baths</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laboratory water baths are used for heating or cooling samples in various scientific applications. Circulating water baths are equipped with a pump to circulate the water, ensuring even temperature distribution. Non-circulating water baths do not have a pump and rely on natural convection for temperature uniformity. Both types have their own advantages and are suitable for different types of experiments. Circulating water baths are ideal for precise temperature control, while non-circulating water baths are simpler in design and more cost-effective for basic heating or cooling needs.</p></p>
