<p><p>Waste oil drain markets can be divided into two types: conventional and self-extraction. Conventional waste oil drains require manual removal of oil from machinery or vehicles using a pump or gravity. On the other hand, self-extraction waste oil drains are designed to automatically extract oil from engines without the need for manual labor. These systems utilize suction technology to efficiently remove oil, making the process quicker and easier for users. Both types of waste oil drains offer solutions for disposing of used oil in a safe and environmentally friendly manner.</p></p>
