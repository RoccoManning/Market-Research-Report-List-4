<p><p>Hot riveting tools are used in various industries for joining two or more materials together using a heated rivet. There are three main types of hot riveting tools in the market: pneumatic riveting tools, battery-operated riveting tools, and hand-operated riveting tools. Pneumatic riveting tools are powered by compressed air and are suitable for heavy-duty applications. Battery-operated riveting tools offer more flexibility and portability. Hand-operated riveting tools are manually operated and are ideal for small-scale projects or areas with limited access to power sources.</p></p>
