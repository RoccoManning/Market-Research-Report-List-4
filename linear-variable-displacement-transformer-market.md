<p><p>The Linear Variable Displacement Transformer (LVDT) market can be divided into two main types: AC Type and DC Type. On the other hand, DC Type LVDTs work on a direct current power supply, making them ideal for applications where the input power is DC. Both types of LVDTs offer precise and accurate displacement measurement, but their specific use cases are determined by the type of power supply available.</p></p>
