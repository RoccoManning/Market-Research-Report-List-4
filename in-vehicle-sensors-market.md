<p><p>In vehicle sensors market consists of two primary types: wired and wireless. Wired sensors are physically connected to the vehicle's systems and components through cables, allowing for reliable and real-time data transmission. On the other hand, wireless sensors transmit data through radio frequency signals, eliminating the need for physical cables and allowing for more flexibility in installation and placement. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in enhancing vehicle safety, efficiency, and connectivity in the automotive industry.</p></p>
