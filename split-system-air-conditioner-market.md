<p><p>A Split System Air Conditioner is a type of air conditioning system in which the compressor unit is located outside the building, while the indoor unit is connected to the outdoor unit through refrigerant lines. This configuration allows for quieter operation inside the building, as well as more efficient cooling or heating of individual rooms.</p><p>The Split System Air Conditioner Market is witnessing rapid growth, with a projected CAGR of 14% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to factors such as increasing urbanization, rising disposable incomes, and the demand for energy-efficient cooling solutions. Additionally, advancements in technology have led to the development of smart and connected split system air conditioners, which are further driving market growth.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the Split System Air Conditioner Market is the increasing adoption of inverter technology. Inverter air conditioners adjust the compressor speed to maintain the desired temperature, resulting in higher energy efficiency and lower electricity bills. Another trend is the growing popularity of split system air conditioners with multi-zone capabilities, allowing users to control the temperature in multiple rooms independently. These trends are expected to continue driving market growth in the coming years.</p></p>
