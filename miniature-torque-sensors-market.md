<p><p>Miniature torque sensors are used to measure torque in various applications. Static torque sensors measure torque in a stationary position, providing accurate readings for applications that do not involve movement. Dynamic torque sensors, on the other hand, measure torque in real-time during rotation or movement, making them suitable for applications requiring continuous monitoring and analysis of torque fluctuations. Both types of sensors play crucial roles in ensuring precision and efficiency in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
