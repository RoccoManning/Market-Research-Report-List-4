<p><strong>The Thermal Oxidizers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Fired Thermal Oxidizer</li><li>Regenerative Thermal Oxidizer (RTO)</li><li>Thermal Recuperative Oxidizer</li><li>Biomass Fired Thermal Oxidizer</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal oxidizers are used for reducing emissions of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and hazardous air pollutants from industrial processes. Different types of thermal oxidizers are available in the market to cater to various needs. Direct fired thermal oxidizers burn VOCs directly in a burner to treat the exhaust gas. Regenerative thermal oxidizers (RTO) use ceramic beds to preheat incoming exhaust gases before combustion. Thermal recuperative oxidizers recover heat from the combustion process to preheat incoming gases. Biomass fired thermal oxidizers use organic materials as a fuel source. Other types of thermal oxidizers include catalytic oxidizers and regenerative catalytic oxidizers.</p></p>
