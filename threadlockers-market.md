<p><p>Threadlockers are categorized based on their strength levels into three main types: Low, Medium, and High. Low strength threadlockers are designed for easy disassembly with hand tools and are commonly used for small fasteners. Medium strength threadlockers offer a balance between ease of disassembly and resistance to vibration and are suitable for a wide range of applications. High strength threadlockers provide the strongest bond and are ideal for critical fastening applications where maximum resistance to loosening is required.</p></p>
