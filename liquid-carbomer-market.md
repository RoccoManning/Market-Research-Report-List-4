<p><p>Liquid Carbomer is a versatile ingredient used in various industries such as cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and personal care products. It is available in two main types: Homopolymer and Copolymer. Homopolymer Carbomer is a single type of monomer polymerized to form a uniform structure, while Copolymer Carbomer is a blend of two different monomers that result in a more varied structure. Both types offer unique viscosity and stability properties, making them suitable for a wide range of applications in the market.</p></p>
