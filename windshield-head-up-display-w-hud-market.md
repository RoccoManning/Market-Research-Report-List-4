<p><ul><li>Front Mounting</li><li>Rear Mounting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Windshield Head-up Display (W-HUD) market is categorized into two types - Front Mounting and Rear Mounting. Front Mounting W-HUD displays information directly onto the windshield in front of the driver for easy viewing. This type is commonly used in modern vehicles to provide essential driving information without distracting the driver. Rear Mounting W-HUD, on the other hand, projects information onto a separate panel near the driver's line of sight behind the steering wheel. Both types aim to enhance driving safety and convenience by providing real-time information to the driver.</p></p>
