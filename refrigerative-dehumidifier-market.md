<p><p>Refrigerative dehumidifiers come in two main types: Tower Type and Rotor Type. Tower Type dehumidifiers utilize a cooling coil to condense moisture from the air, while Rotor Type dehumidifiers use a rotating desiccant wheel to absorb moisture. Both types are effective in removing excess humidity from the air, with Tower Type dehumidifiers typically being more compact and suitable for smaller spaces, while Rotor Type dehumidifiers are known for their high efficiency and low energy consumption. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
