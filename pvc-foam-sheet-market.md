<p><strong>The PVC Foam Sheet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Cell Type</li><li>Closed Cell Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PVC foam sheets are categorized into two main types based on cell structure: open cell and closed cell. Open cell foam sheets have interconnected cells, allowing air and moisture to pass through easily. This type is ideal for applications where cushioning and flexibility are needed. On the other hand, closed cell foam sheets have individual cells that are sealed off, making them impermeable to air and moisture. These sheets provide better insulation and are resistant to water and chemicals. Each type caters to different market needs and applications.</p></p>
