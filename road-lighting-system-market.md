<p><p>Road lighting systems can be categorized into two types: wired and wireless. Wired road lighting systems require a physical connection between the fixtures and power source, providing a reliable and consistent lighting solution. On the other hand, wireless road lighting systems use technology such as Bluetooth or Wi-Fi to communicate between fixtures and the control system, offering a more flexible and easily adaptable solution. Both types have their own unique advantages and are used in different scenarios based on the specific needs of the project.</p></p>
