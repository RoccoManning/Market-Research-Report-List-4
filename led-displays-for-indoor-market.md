<p><p>LED Displays for Indoor Market Types can be classified into two main categories: Surface Mounted and Individually Mounted. Surface Mounted LEDs are assembled on a printed circuit board and are commonly used in large display screens for indoor advertising. On the other hand, Individually Mounted LEDs are small units that are mounted individually on a display board, allowing for greater flexibility in creating dynamic and customizable indoor displays. Both types offer high brightness, energy efficiency, and long lifespan, making them popular choices for indoor marketing.</p></p>
