<p><strong>The Living Insecticide Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microbial Pesticides</li><li>Plant Pesticides</li><li>Biochemical Pesticides</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Living insecticide market consists of three main types: microbial pesticides, plant pesticides, and biochemical pesticides. Microbial pesticides are derived from naturally occurring microorganisms and are used to control pests. Plant pesticides are extracted from plants and have insecticidal properties. Biochemical pesticides are substances naturally produced by plants or animals that interfere with the pest's biological processes. Each type of living insecticide offers environmentally friendly alternatives to traditional chemical pesticides and are gaining popularity in the market.</p></p>
