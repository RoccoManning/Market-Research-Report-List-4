<p><p>Handy slit lamps are ophthalmic instruments used for eye examinations. The market offers two main types: monocular slit lamps and binocular slit lamps. Monocular slit lamps have only one eyepiece, providing a cost-effective option for basic eye examinations. On the other hand, binocular slit lamps feature two eyepieces, offering improved depth perception and better viewing for more detailed examinations. Both types of slit lamps play a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring various eye conditions, catering to different preferences and needs of ophthalmologists.</p></p>
