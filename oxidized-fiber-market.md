<p><p>Oxidized fiber market is categorized into two main types: Continuous Fiber and Non-continuous Fiber. Continuous Fiber refers to fibers that are long and uniform in structure, providing high strength and durability. Non-continuous Fiber, on the other hand, consists of shorter fibers that are not uniform in length. While Continuous Fiber is commonly used in applications requiring high tensile strength such as aerospace and automotive industries, Non-continuous Fiber is often utilized in less demanding applications like construction and insulation.</p></p>
