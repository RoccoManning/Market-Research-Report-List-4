<p><p>Polymeric membranes are classified into Solution-Diffusion Membrane and Ion Membrane market types. Solution-Diffusion Membranes are made of polymers that allow the transport of specific molecules based on their solubility and diffusion rates. On the other hand, Ion Membranes are designed to selectively transport ions while blocking other substances. These membranes are widely used in various applications such as water treatment, gas separation, and fuel cells due to their ability to separate and purify substances effectively.</p></p>
