<p><p>PVDC latex is commonly used in various industries for its barrier properties and adhesion. Two main types of PVDC latex in the market are emulsion PVDC latex and solution PVDC latex. Emulsion PVDC latex is dispersed in water and used for applications such as coatings and adhesives. On the other hand, solution PVDC latex is dissolved in a solvent and utilized in applications requiring high clarity and barrier properties, such as food packaging. Both types offer excellent barrier properties against gases and moisture, making them ideal choices for various industries.</p></p>
