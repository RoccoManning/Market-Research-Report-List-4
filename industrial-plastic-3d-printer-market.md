<p><strong>The Industrial Plastic 3D Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Selective Laser Melting (SLM)</li><li>Electronic Beam Melting (EBM)</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Selective Laser Melting (SLM) is a type of industrial plastic 3D printing that uses a high-powered laser to selectively melt layers of plastic powder, creating complex and detailed parts with high accuracy. Electronic Beam Melting (EBM) is another method that uses an electron beam to melt and fuse the plastic powder together. Both SLM and EBM are commonly used in aerospace, automotive, and medical industries. Other types of industrial plastic 3D printing methods include Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM), Stereolithography (SLA), and Digital Light Processing (DLP), each with its own unique advantages and applications.</p></p>
