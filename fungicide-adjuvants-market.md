<p><p>Fungicide adjuvants can be categorized into two main types: single function adjuvants and multifunction adjuvants. Single function adjuvants are designed to serve a specific purpose, such as improving fungicide coverage or enhancing penetration into plant tissues. On the other hand, multifunction adjuvants are more versatile and can perform multiple functions, such as improving adhesion, spreadability, and rainfastness of fungicides. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the effectiveness and efficiency of fungicide applications in agriculture.</p></p>
