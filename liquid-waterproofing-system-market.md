<p><p>Liquid waterproofing systems are categorized into various types based on their composition and properties. PMMA or polymethyl methacrylate is known for its rapid curing and strong adhesion properties. Polyurethane systems offer flexibility and abrasion resistance while SEBS (styrene-ethylene-butylene-styrene) provides excellent elongation and durability. Other options in the market include acrylic, bituminous, and silicone-based systems. These different types cater to diverse waterproofing needs and offer a range of features such as UV resistance, chemical resistance, and ease of application.</p></p>
